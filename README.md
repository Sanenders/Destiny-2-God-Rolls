# Destiny 2 God Roll Wishlist 2.0

This repository contains a wishlist for Destiny 2 weapon God Rolls. Most God Rolls come from Aegis Endgame Analysis Spreadsheet.

## Important

**All strong perks have been marked as God Roll perks.**  
However, even with a God Roll designation, Perk 1 and Perk 2 may not always synergize perfectly. It is highly recommended to review the full **weapon details** to evaluate the best possible combinations for your playstyle.

### Scope of This List
- This wishlist focuses primarily on weapons that were **added or significantly updated** with the *Monument to Triumph* update.
- Older versions of weapons (e.g., the Season 27 Mint Retrograde) are no longer marked, as newer and improved versions have been released.

### Version Information
- Unlike the **God Roll Wishlist 1.0**, this version (2.0) has been completely rebuilt from the ground up and is **PvE-focused**.
- It contains **no PvP rolls**.

### Masterwork Recommendations
Suggested Masterwork types have been intentionally removed from the notes, as they are typically obvious for most weapons (e.g., Charge Time for Fusion Rifles).

## Known Bugged Weapons

A small number of weapons are currently unable to receive certain strong perks. These are temporarily adjusted as follows:

- **Mykel's Revenge** — Adept missing one Perk (temporarely switched to *Dimensional Shift*).
- **Praedyth's Revenge** — Normal version is missing *Aggregate Charge* (temporarily replaced with *Envious Arsenal*).
- **Acasia's Dejection** — Also missing one perk (the missing perk is generally considered weak anyway).

These weapons will be corrected once Bungie releases a fix.

## How to use the Wishlist on the Little Light App (prefered)

1. Download the Little Light App:<br>
Mobile: <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=me.markezine.luzinha">Android Play Store</a> / <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/little-light-for-destiny-2/id1373037254?ls=1&mt=8">iOS App Store</a><br>
Desktop: <a href="https://apps.microsoft.com/detail/9pmg9zq41k95?hl=de-DE&gl=DE">Microsoft App Store</a>

2. Use your Platform Account to sign into Bungie.net
3. Choose your language and skip the wishlist selection
4. Go to settings and click on **add wishlist**
5. Click on **Custom** and paste the following link:
```bash
https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Sanenders/Destiny-2-God-Rolls/refs/heads/main/littlelight.json
```
6. Press the button **Load Wishlist** and add the wishlist to the Little Light App.
