# Destiny 2 God Roll Wishlist

This repository contains a wish list for Destiny 2 weapon God Rolls. Most God Rolls come from popular websites such as blueberries.gg.

## Important

- It's possible to add “good weapon perks,” even if the weapon is not a God Roll. Only God Rolls are listed in this wish list.

- Crafted weapons may not be recognized as God Rolls, even though upon closer inspection, the weapon does contain the correct perks.

- Parameters for masterworks cannot be transferred. Instead, you should take a look at the wish list notes. The required masterwork is listed there.
